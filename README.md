# Chronos
Original: This app is for B2B booking system. 

Current branches:
1. For logging the income/expense, customer can easilty control the money flow in a family or even in a group. The target audience for this is a person who is in charge of family's or group's income/expense.
2. For connecting the LINE API, this is for business, they can manage their customers' reservation easily and arrange the shift of the reservations of the customers.
